Our History as Builders Software Enterprises

Rick Powell - Founder of BSE

On August 20th, 1990 Rick Powell founded Builders Software Enterprises. That was the date Rick Powell sold his first estimating software program to a homebuilder in Fort Worth, Texas. Rick's ten year experience as a homebuilder gave him the background to create a software system that would work logically for a builder. He then spent the next 20+ years teaching homebuilders how to ditch their pencils while sharpening their numbers when it comes to estimating the price of building a home, coupled with using QuickBooksÂ® as the job cost and general ledger accounting solution.

How Rick and I Got Started

Tony Vance - CEO of Builder Professional

I'm Tony Vance, CEO of Builder Professional, and I first went to work for Rick in 1999 as a software developer for my first professional job in the industry. Rick met me and decided to give me a shot, and we went right to work on the BSE Estimator 2000. The BSE Estimator 2000 was released in February of 2000, and a long-time partnership was formed. We also built the next two versions of the BSE Estimator together over the years. At the same time, I worked in various positions in software development and management, garnering 15 years of industry experience and preparation for the next steps in this company's evolution.

We Are Now Builder Professional

Very sadly, in May of 2015, my friend Rick Powell passed away unexpectedly. A lot of our customers knew Rick well, and most people that knew Rick liked him a lot. We started Builder Professional to carry on the work that he started and continue to serve homebuilders and their needs. Rick asked me many times to carry on this legacy after he retired, so when he passed unexpectedly, I felt like we should keep going. Rick's daughter Carmen agreed, and after some planning and careful thought, Builder Professional was born.

We are proud to work with the great team that Rick had already assembled and cultivated, and I am confident that not only does Builder Professional come from a great BSE tradition, but Builder Professional will continue to push forward.
